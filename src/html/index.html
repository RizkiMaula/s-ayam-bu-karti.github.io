OUR ICON

Meet Rocky the Rooster, the most masculine rooster you'll ever know. With his vibrant plumage and confident strut, Rocky is more than just a bird; he's a legend, and he's the proud ambassador of Soto Ayam Bu Karti, the beloved restaurant known for its delectable chicken soup and traditional Indonesian cuisine. Rocky wants you to experience the rich flavors and comforting warmth of Soto Ayam Bu Karti, where every bowl of soto ayam is a testament to time-honored recipes and meticulous preparation. The broth, simmered to perfection with herbs and spices, the tender pieces of chicken, and the fresh garnishes create a symphony of flavors that is both hearty and satisfying. But don't take Rocky's word for it—come and taste for yourself. Rocky makes it clear that enjoying a meal at Soto Ayam Bu Karti is not just a suggestion—it's a command. If you dare to ignore his invitation, be prepared to face his determined pursuit, for Rocky will hunt you down with the persistence only a rooster of his caliber can muster. His mission is simple: to ensure everyone gets a taste of the culinary excellence at Soto Ayam Bu Karti. So heed his call, and savor the best soto ayam in town—Rocky insists!

"Pok pok pok kok kok kok kooook" he said. It means "EAT YOUR SOTO"
